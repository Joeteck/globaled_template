Cookie Policy

Last updated: December 2025

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how GlobalEd and Tour Inc. uses them, and how you can control them. We are committed to transparency about our tracking technologies in compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and other data protection regulations.

Understanding Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

Cookies typically contain:

  • Name of the server: The server that placed the cookie
  • Unique ID: An identifier for your browser
  • Expiry date: When the cookie will be deleted
  • Value: The information the cookie stores

Cookies help websites remember your preferences, understand how you use the site, and improve your browsing experience. They do not contain viruses or malicious software.

Cookie Categories We Use

We use different types of cookies for various purposes. Below are the categories of cookies used on our website:

Essential Cookies

Always Active

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas.

  • Session management
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Load balancing
  • User authentication

Duration: Session or up to 24 hours

Analytics Cookies

Optional

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. They are only loaded with your explicit consent.

  • Page view tracking
  • Visitor count and demographics
  • Popular content analysis
  • User journey optimization

Duration: Up to 2 years

Functional Cookies

Optional

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization. They may be set by us or by third-party providers.

  • Cookie consent preferences (stored in localStorage)
  • Form auto-completion
  • Video player preferences
  • Social media integration

Duration: Up to 1 year

Important Note About Analytics

Google Analytics cookies are only loaded when you explicitly consent to analytics cookies through our cookie banner. If you reject non-essential cookies or decline analytics cookies, Google Analytics will not be loaded on your browser.

We use Google Analytics with IP anonymization enabled, meaning your IP address is truncated before being sent to Google's servers for processing.

Third-Party Cookies: Some cookies may be placed by third-party services embedded on our site, such as social media buttons or embedded videos. These third parties have their own privacy policies.

Managing Cookie Preferences

How to Control Cookies

You have several options to control and manage cookies:

1. Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie consent banner that allows you to:

  • Accept All: Accept all cookies (essential, analytics, functional)
  • Customize: Choose which cookie categories to accept
  • Reject Non-Essential: Accept only essential cookies

2. Browser Settings

You can also control cookies through your web browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:
