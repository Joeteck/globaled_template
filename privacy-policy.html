Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

This Privacy Policy explains how GlobalEd & Tour Inc. ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use our website and services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in our data practices.

1 Who We Are

Data Controller: GlobalEd & Tour Inc.
Contact: info@globaledtour.com
Address: Jacob Town, Paynesville, Monrovia, Liberia.

As the data controller, we are responsible for determining how and why your personal data is collected and processed in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

2 What Data We Collect

We collect and process the following categories of personal data:

  • Contact Details: Full name, email address, phone number (if provided)
  • Academic Information: Educational background, interests, application data
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited
  • Communication Data: Messages, inquiries, and feedback you send to us
  • Marketing Preferences: Your choices regarding communications

We collect this data through website forms, email communications, and automated technologies.

3 Why We Use Your Data

We process your personal data for the following purposes and legal bases:

Service Delivery To respond to inquiries, provide consultations, and process applications (Contract)
Website Improvement To enhance user experience and website functionality (Legitimate Interests)
Legal Compliance To meet regulatory requirements and legal obligations (Legal Obligation)
Marketing Communications To send relevant updates with your consent (Consent)

4 Data Protection & Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Access controls and authentication measures
  • Staff training on data protection practices

While we strive to protect your personal data, no electronic transmission or storage method is 100% secure. We continuously review and enhance our security measures.

5 Data Sharing & International Transfers

We may share your data with:

  • Service Providers: Email delivery, hosting, analytics, and form processing partners
  • Partner Institutions: Universities and colleges when you authorize application submission
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our rights

International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed outside your country of residence. When this occurs, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (EU/EEA transfers)
  • Adequacy decisions where applicable
  • Additional organizational and technical measures

6 Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Retention periods include:

  • Active inquiries: 2 years from last contact
  • Application data: 5 years from completion
  • Legal obligations: As required by applicable laws
  • Marketing consent: Until withdrawal of consent

7 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Access Right to access your personal data
Correction Right to correct inaccurate data
Deletion Right to request data deletion
Objection Right to object to processing

Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of your data protection rights, contact us at:

Email: info@globaledllc.com

We respond to all legitimate requests within one month. If you have concerns about our data practices, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

9 Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to delete such information.

10 Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, services, or legal requirements. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

Notification of Changes: We will notify you of any material changes through our website or via email when appropriate.
